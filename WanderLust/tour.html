Destination

This premium tour package is designed for the curious traveller who wants to experience the best of this destination without the usual hassle. Every day is thoughtfully curated to balance guided exploration with free time to wander on your own.

Group Size

Max 12 travelers per group for a personalised experience

Languages

Tours available in English, Spanish, French and German

Fitness Level

Moderate — suitable for most able-bodied adults

Age Range

Suitable for travelers aged 12 and above

Day 1 — Arrival & Welcome

Arrive at your destination and transfer to your luxury accommodation. Enjoy a welcome dinner and briefing session with your WanderLust guide.

Day 2 — Iconic Highlights Tour

Full-day guided tour of the must-see landmarks. Enjoy a scenic viewpoint picnic lunch with panoramic views.

Day 3 — Cultural Immersion

Visit local markets, meet artisans, and take a cooking class with a local chef. Evening free for personal exploration.

Day 4 — Adventure Day

Optional adventure activities (hiking, snorkelling, or cycling). Relaxed afternoon by the water.

Day 5 — Hidden Gems

Off-the-beaten-path excursion discovering secret spots most tourists never see. Sunset cocktail hour.

Day 6 — Leisure & Farewell

Free morning for shopping or relaxation. Farewell group dinner at a top-rated local restaurant.

Day 7 — Departure

Transfer to the airport. Your WanderLust adventure ends here — until the next one!
